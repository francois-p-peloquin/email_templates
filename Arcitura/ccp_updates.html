CCP 1, 2, 3 - Update 2.1

On October 15, 2012, Cloud School announced several new enhancements to the Cloud Certified Professional (CCP) program and specifically, CCP Course Modules 1, 2, and 3. The increased scope in content will further enrich the CCP learning and exam preparation experience.

New versions of Self-Study Guides for CCP Modules 1, 2 and 3 with over 50 pages of text book references and mapping tables added

For any further questions regarding this update or the CCP program, contact info@cloudschool.com.


New 300+ Page CCP Supplement

The Cloud Computing: Concepts & Technology CCP Supplement is the result of years of research and analysis (conducted by Arcitura Education in conjunction with the University of Brasilia and the University of Derby) of the commercial cloud computing industry, cloud computing vendor platforms, and further innovation and contributions made by cloud computing industry standards organizations and practitioners.

The purpose of this supplement is to break down proven and mature cloud computing technologies and practices into a series of well-defined concepts, models, and technology mechanisms. The range of topics covered are documented using vendor-neutral terms and descriptions that are carefully defined to ensure alignment with the cloud computing industry as a whole.

Version 2.1 of the CCP Course Booklets and Self-Study Guides for Modules 1, 2 and 3 have been updated to fully incorporate relevant parts of the CCP Supplement.
